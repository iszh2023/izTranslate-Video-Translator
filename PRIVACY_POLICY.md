izTranslate Video Translator Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-15

Summary
- izTranslate Video Translator helps you read video captions/subtitles and optionally speaks them aloud using your browser’s built-in text-to-speech.

Data we collect
- We do not collect, sell, or share personal data.
- We do not transmit your caption text, browsing history, or page content to any external server.

Data processed on your device
- The extension reads caption/subtitle text from the current page (for example, on-screen captions or the video’s text track) only to display it in the sidebar and (optionally) speak it aloud.
- Caption text is processed locally on your device and is not sent off-device by this extension.

Data stored on your device
The extension stores only user preferences using Chrome extension storage, such as:
- Whether reading/speaking is enabled
- Caption source selection (auto / on-screen / track)
- Speech rate and target language settings
- Audio mix / sync options
- Sidebar width and UI settings

Third-party services
- None.

Remote code
- The extension does not load or execute remote code (no external scripts, no eval).
